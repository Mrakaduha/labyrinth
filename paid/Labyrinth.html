Labyrinth

A simple tool to interrupt the constant stream of thoughts. The shape of the Chartres labyrinth adds a quiet sense of wonder.

It is traditionally seen as a path through life, but you don’t need to hold onto any meaning.

Just follow the ball and notice what you feel.

Install on your device.
No account. No tracking.
Works fully offline.
